  • Power System Dynamics 1 ‚Äì Nonlinear modeling and control of a DC generator and a synchronous machine in MATLAB/Simulink; parameter identification, PID-based speed/voltage control, AVR and governor design, and validation via step responses and operating-point linearization.

  • Power System Utilization ‚Äì Optimal energy scheduling in a microgrid using a hybrid PSO‚ÄìANN approach on the IEEE-14 bus system; incorporation of realistic load profiles, prices, and weather data; training with Levenberg‚ÄìMarquardt and performance comparison with BPSO.
