Work Experience

  • Research Assistant, Amirkabir University of Technology, Iran, 2023–2025
    During my thesis research at the FACTS laboratory, I collaborated closely with graduate and doctoral students, focusing on optimizing the use of Mobile Battery Energy Storage Systems (MBESS) within microgrids. I developed and tested a novel multi-objective optimization framework using meta-heuristic algorithms to efficiently manage MBESS placement and operational performance in a 33-bus distribution network. The results demonstrated that MBESS not only reduced operational costs and power losses, but also significantly improved voltage stability compared to traditional fixed storage systems. A key innovation in my research was the integration of autonomous electric trucks for battery transport, which was analyzed alongside the uncertainties of renewable energy sources and load variations.
  • Internship, Regional Electricity Company of Mazandaran Province, Iran, 2019
    During a three-month internship, I had the opportunity to learn about various types of transformers, cables, and low-voltage lines. Additionally, I gained practical experience using AutoCAD software, where I was instructed in wiring and positioning electric poles, as well as creating drawings for optimal power transmission.
